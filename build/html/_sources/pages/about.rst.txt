About
=====

General ideas
===================

* **The main idea is connect the client with a chatbot and operator inside the same window.**

* **easy integration into any web page.**

* **conversation data protected.**

* **easy flow of information inside.**



How it works
============

.. image:: ../../images/general_connection_scheme.jpg
  :width: 600
  :alt: general connection scheme

The customer enters the web page, opens the window and writes to the chatbot. In case the customer wants to speak to a real person, he presses a button and the system connects him to an operator.
